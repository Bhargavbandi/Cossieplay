We are a small family-run local business, operating in Hulkstown since 2012, hoping to improve our business by getting on the Internet. We create custom cosplay and fancy dress costumes and run a monthly cosplay party. We get about 30 people to the parties each month and make about 4 costumes per week – not enough. The website should help us be more successful at these. Dressing up is fun, good for the socially awkward and very creative.
